Kottayam

   Bordered by the lofty and mighty Western Ghats on the east and the Vembanad Lake and paddy fields of Kuttanad on the west, Kottayam is a land of unique characteristics. Panoramic backwater stretches, lush paddy fields, highlands, hills and hillocks, extensive rubber plantations, places associated with many legends and a high literacy rate have given Kottayam District the enviable title: The land of letters, legends, latex and lakes. The city is an important trading center of spices and commercial crops, especially rubber. Most of India’s natural rubber originates from the acres of well-kept plantations of Kottayam, also home to the Rubber Board. Kottayam is also called as “Akshara Nagari” which means the “city of letters” considering its contribution to print media and literature.


Best places to visit

Marmala Waterfalls

Located a few kilometers from Erattupetta, the Marmala Waterfalls is the best location for an adventure-seeking tourist. Though the journey to the waterfalls is quite arduous and demanding, the transfixing beauty it offers is rewarding. Aside from the peace and calm aura the Marmala Waterfalls offers, the view of the shimmering waters cascading down clear and bright rocks is simply beguiling.

Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary

Seated magnificently by the banks of the Vembanad Lake, the Kumarakom bird sanctuary is home to a plethora of rare, colorful, and good to look at bird species. Stretched over 14 acres, tourists can sit on the expanse of canopied lush green fields while watching these colorful birds in flight. The sanctuary offers delight to honeymooners, picnic lovers, and also to tourists and visitors seeking a calm and peaceful location to unwind and relax.

Mozart Art Gallery

This well-maintained private art gallery situated in Aymanam is a getaway location for all art lovers and enthusiasts. Adding to the glory of this gallery is its exhibition of special mythological and religious drawings from Kerala, antiques, and other cultural-historic products.

Illikkal Kallu

Being one of the highest peaks in Kottayam, the view of sunrise and sunset from the peak is magical. Demonstrating the raw beauty of rocky mountains, this natural peak’s serenity and calm aura is soothing and refreshing. Hiking, trekking, and picnicking is a regular activity at this paradisiacal summit.

Nattakom And Panachikkad

Well known for their peace and calm, Nattakom and Panachikkad are two secluded villages situated a few kilometers from the city. Though private and secluded, these villages nonetheless offer views of special migratory birds and fascinating landscapes. With the right mix of heritage, culture, and religion, these villages are homes to some of the best temples in the city.
